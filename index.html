1What counts as a contribution
📝

Educational thread

Break down a Somnia feature, mechanism, or roadmap update. Original analysis people learn from.

🎨

Original art or media

Art, animation, design, memes with clear Somnia identity. A series or recurring style is especially valued.

🛠️

Tools and dashboards

Something you built that helps the community: trackers, calculators, dashboards, bots, sites.

📚

Tutorials and guides

How-to content, walkthroughs, onboarding for newcomers, docs that fill a real gap.

🎬

Video or audio

Explainers, walkthroughs, recap videos, podcast episodes, Twitter Spaces summaries.

🤝

Consistent helpfulness

A pattern of answering newcomer questions, debugging in support, being the go-to person in a channel.

Pro tip: one summary thread per week beats spamming individual posts. Bundle everything you did that week into a single forum thread. Mods see the full picture and can stack a bigger bonus on top of the base +3 if your week was outstanding. Quality and rhythm both count.
What doesn't count: low-effort memes with no Somnia context, "gm" posts, copy-pasted content, repeated noise. Mods just won't react.

7FAQ
What is REP and why does it matter?
REP is an identity signal, not a currency. You can't spend it. The number is visible proof you've contributed to Somnia consistently, and it's how the community separates real long-term contributors from noise. The harder it is to earn, the more it means to hold.
How do I become a Guardian?
1) Post in #contribution-board in Discord. Each post that gets 4 distinct mods reacting ✅ counts toward your tally.

2) Once you have 10 approved posts, you can submit the Apply for Guardian quest.

3) A mod reviews your application.

4) When a slot opens (cap of 200), a mod runs /rep guardian and you get the role + your first REP.
How does the monthly SOMI payout work?
On the 1st of every month, the top 3 Guardians by REP earned in the last 30 days get SOMI: 500 / 350 / 250. It's ranked by earnings in the window, not by total balance, so a fresh Guardian with a hot month can beat a quiet veteran. Winners sit out the next month so the spotlight rotates.
What happens if two people tie for a top-3 spot?
The reward for that spot is split equally between everyone tied. If two Guardians tie for 2nd, they each get 175 SOMI (350 / 2). If three tie for 1st, they each get ~166 SOMI (500 / 3). When a tie happens, the team handles tipping manually so the math is clean and verifiable. The auto-payout pauses on ties and pings the admins.
What happens if I go inactive?
If you earn nothing on a given day, you lose 0.5 REP to decay. If you go 60 days with no credit, your balance is halved AND your Guardian role is revoked. You'd then need to re-apply through the normal Apply for Guardian flow. The halved balance is preserved, so coming back isn't starting from zero.
Can I lose REP through penalties?
Yes. Mods can apply two penalty types via /rep penalty: spam (-3 REP) for low-effort or repeated noise, and misleading (-10 REP) for false info that could harm users. Balances floor at 0 and never go negative. Harassment is handled through normal Discord moderation, not the REP system.
What is the BETA role?
A permanent badge for the 125 community members who were active before REP v2 launched. It's an "I was here first" signal. BETA holders get exclusive perks over time, like events, raffles, and drops planned just for them. The badge is permanent and never revoked.
Is there a cap on REP?
Yes, balances are capped at 400 REP. Once you hit it, you stop accruing until decay or a penalty brings you back under. This keeps the leaderboard meaningful and prevents long-time Guardians from running away with the score forever.
Earned, not given. Active, not historical.
The number itself is the prize - SOMI is the cherry, not the cake.
